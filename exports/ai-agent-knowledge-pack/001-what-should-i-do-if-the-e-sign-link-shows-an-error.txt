Customer Scenario: What should I do if the e-sign link shows an error?
Scenario Title: What should I do if the e-sign link shows an error?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: e-sign, error, application completed, card declined, underwriting, cache, portal

Answer To Give The Customer:
Check the account stage first:
•	If at "Application Completed": The customer must authorize their debit card via the online portal at www.ticktockloans.com
•	If at "Card Declined": They need to reattempt verification via the portal
•	If at "Underwriting": The application is under review
•	If still at "e-sign": Test the link. If the issue persists, ask the customer to clear their cache and try again. Escalate with a screenshot if necessary.
