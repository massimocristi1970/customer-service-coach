Scenario Title: I've received the transaction response: "Transaction Success" -- what does this mean?
Category: Transaction Responses & Payment Issues
Keywords: transaction success, payment, verification, successful

Answer To Give The Customer:
This means that the transaction verification or payment was successful. Either the payment has been taken, or the verification has completed as expected.
