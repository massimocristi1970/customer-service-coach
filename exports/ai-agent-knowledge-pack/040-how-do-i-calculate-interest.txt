Customer Scenario: How do I calculate interest?
Scenario Title: How do I calculate interest?
Category: Collections & Account Status Management
Keywords: interest, calculate, work out, how much, charge, daily interest, price cap

Answer To Give The Customer:
Convert the percentage to a decimal:

0.8% = 0.008

Multiply the decimal by the amount:

£100 × 0.008 = £0.80

💡 Final Answer:

0.8% of £100 is £0.80 (per day)
