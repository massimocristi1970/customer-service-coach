Customer Scenario: How do you update the card details?
Scenario Title: How do you update the card details?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: Update card, card, pre-auth, authorisation, new card, securely, banking app

Answer To Give The Customer:
To update their card details, the customer must complete the process via the website. Here's how to guide them:

Ask the customer to visit the website and log in to their account.

Once logged in, they should look for either:
• “Update Card Securely”, or
• “My Card”

Clicking on either option will take them to the card update page - if they do not see this get the customer to refresh the page.

The customer should now enter their new card information on the page provided.

After entering the details, the customer will be prompted to verify the transaction in their banking app.

🔹 Step 5: Return to Website
Once verified, the customer must return to the original website page.

This final step ensures the update is sent successfully through our API and applied to their account.
