Customer Scenario: What is the cooling-off period?
Scenario Title: What is the cooling-off period?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: cooling, between loans, period

Answer To Give The Customer:
The cooling-off period is a set amount of time that must pass between loans once a previous loan has been repaid. During this time, any new applications will be automatically declined, in line with our repeat lending policy.
The current cooling-off period is 5 days from the date the last loan was repaid.
