Customer Scenario: Do we offer top-up loans?
Scenario Title: Do we offer top-up loans?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: top-up, top, second loan, additional loan, increase balance

Answer To Give The Customer:
No, we do not offer top-up loans. Customers are also subject to a cooling-off period which is 5 days from the date the last loan was repaid.. If a customer wishes to repay their loan and reapply, they will need to take this condition into account.
