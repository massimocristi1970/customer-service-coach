Customer Scenario: How do I request a password?
Scenario Title: How do I request a password?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: password, reset, login, website, online, forgot

Answer To Give The Customer:
To reset a customer’s online password, go to our website at www.ticktockloans.com and click “Login” in the top right corner. Instead of logging in, select “Forgot password” underneath. Enter the customer’s email address and surname exactly as they appear in our CRM; if the details don’t match, the reset won’t work. A new password will be sent to the email address on file.
