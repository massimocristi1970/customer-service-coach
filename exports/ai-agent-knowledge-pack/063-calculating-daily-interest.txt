Customer Scenario: The customer is asking about Calculating Daily Interest.
Scenario Title: Calculating Daily Interest
Category: Transaction Responses & Payment Issues
Keywords: calculate, interest, daily interest, charge, queries

Answer To Give The Customer:
Purpose:
To provide staff with a clear process for explaining how interest is applied and to calculate the daily interest charge for customer accounts.

Interest Rate:

We charge 0.8% per day on the outstanding principal balance.

Daily Interest Calculation:

Identify the outstanding principal on the customer account.

Multiply the principal by 0.8% (0.008) to calculate the daily interest charge.

Formula:

Daily Interest = Outstanding Principal × 0.008

Example Calculations:

Principal (£)	Daily Interest (£)
300	            2.40
500	            4.00
750	            6.00
1,000	        8.00
1,500	        12.00

Using the Table/Formula on Calls:

If a customer asks how much interest will be applied each day, staff can either:

Use the table for quick reference, or

Apply the formula to calculate the exact amount for the outstanding balance.

Notes:

Interest is calculated daily based on the current outstanding principal.

Do not include previously accrued interest when calculating daily interest — only use the current principal.

Ensure all calculations and explanations are clearly noted in the customer account.
