Customer Scenario: The customer is asking about Cancelling a Schedule.
Scenario Title: Cancelling a Schedule
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: undo, cancel CPA, cancel PP, cancel schedule, cancel

Answer To Give The Customer:
Purpose:
To explain how agents can cancel a customer’s payment schedule and when this happens automatically.

Steps for Agents (Manual Cancellation):

Go to the account page of the customer’s account.

At the top of the page, locate the orange box titled “Schedules.”

Open the drop-down menu.

Select “Cancel Pay Plan.”

This will immediately cancel the active schedule on the account.

Automatic Cancellations:
A payment schedule will also cancel automatically if the account is moved into any of the following stages:

DM (Debt Management)

Vul (Vulnerability)

Important Notes for Staff:

Do not attempt to restart or amend a schedule without following the correct setup procedure.
