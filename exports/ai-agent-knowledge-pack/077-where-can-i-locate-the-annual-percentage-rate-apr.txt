Customer Scenario: The customer is asking about Where can I locate the Annual Percentage Rate (APR).
Scenario Title: Where can I locate the Annual Percentage Rate (APR)
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: representative, apr, credit, charge, locate, find

Answer To Give The Customer:
Locating the APR Figure

You may receive questions from customers about where to find the APR (Annual Percentage Rate). The location depends on whether the customer is making a general enquiry or already has an active account.

1. General Public / Customer Service Enquiry

For general queries from potential customers who do not yet have an account, the APR can be found on our website.

At the bottom of the website, we display a representative example showing a typical loan and its associated costs:

Example:
Borrow £350 and pay £127.28 per month for 5 months at a fixed interest rate of 0.8% per day (292% per year).
The total charge for credit is £286.40 (all interest).
The total amount repayable is £636.40.
Representative APR: 1297.89%.

This example helps customers understand the cost of borrowing and how the APR relates to our short-term lending model.

2. Existing Customers with an Account

If the customer already has an account with us, the exact APR and full loan details are available within their Consumer Credit Agreement.

This document can be found in the customer’s account area under “Attachments.”
You should guide the customer to this section and explain that the agreement outlines their specific loan terms, including:

The interest rate

The total charge for credit

The total amount repayable

The representative APR
