Customer Scenario: What is a Notice of Sum in Arrears
Scenario Title: What is a Notice of Sum in Arrears
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: NOSIA, sum in arrears, notice, regulated document, notice of sum

Answer To Give The Customer:
A Notice of Sum in Arrears is a formal letter sent by a lender/creditor when a borrower has fallen behind on their repayments for a credit agreement regulated by the Consumer Credit Act (for example: loans, credit cards, hire purchase, store cards).

The notice is issued when the borrower has missed the equivalent of two or more payments, or has underpaid to the point that the arrears reach the value of two full instalments.

Its purpose is to:

Inform the borrower that they are behind on payments

Confirm the total amount of arrears owed

Explain any consequences, such as further action if the arrears are not cleared

Provide required information and protections, including where to get free debt advice
