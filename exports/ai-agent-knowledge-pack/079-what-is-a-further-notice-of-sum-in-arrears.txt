Customer Scenario: What is a Further Notice of Sum in Arrears?
Scenario Title: What is a Further Notice of Sum in Arrears?
Category: Collections & Account Status Management
Keywords: Further notice, FNOSIA, sum in arrears

Answer To Give The Customer:
A further NOSIA is sent every six months if:

The borrower still has arrears outstanding, and

The credit agreement is still active.

Its purpose is to:

Update the borrower on how much arrears remain

Remind them that the account is still behind on payments

Provide the required statutory information, including consumer rights and where to access free debt advice

Just like the initial NOSIA, a further NOSIA:

Is not a court order

Does not mean legal action has begun

Is a legal requirement for regulated credit agreements

It is simply a periodic reminder that arrears are still owed.
