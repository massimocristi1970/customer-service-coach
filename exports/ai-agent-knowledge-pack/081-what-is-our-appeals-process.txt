Customer Scenario: What is our appeals process?
Scenario Title: What is our appeals process?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: appeal, declined, unhappy with decision, appeal process

Answer To Give The Customer:
The appeal is reviewed by a senior underwriter to determine whether the original lending decision was correct.

The process typically takes up to 48 hours from the time the appeal is submitted.

To submit an appeal, the customer must follow the instructions in their decline email. In short, they should:

Email underwriting@ticktockloans.com

Clearly state their concerns and reasons for requesting a review

The senior underwriter will consider the customer’s points as part of their review.

Appeal decisions will be communicated via email.

Customers can submit an appeal either directly by email or manually through a customer service representative.
