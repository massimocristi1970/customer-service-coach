Scenario Title: Can I change my password on my account?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: password update, change password, password

Answer To Give The Customer:
Yes. You can update your password at any time by logging into your online account at www.ticktockloans.com.

Once logged in, go to the “My Profile” section, where you will be able to change your password to something more memorable and secure.
