Scenario Title: Can I change the email address or phone number on theaccount?
Category: Application Processing & General Operations
Keywords: mobile number, updating contact details, telephone number

Answer To Give The Customer:
Yes. You can update the contact details; however, for security reasons we must first speak to the customer on the mobile number currently registered on the account.

Once we’ve verified the customers identity through that number, we can override the existing details and update the email address or phone number.
