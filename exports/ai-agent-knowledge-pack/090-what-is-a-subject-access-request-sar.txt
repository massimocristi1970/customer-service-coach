Customer Scenario: What is a Subject Access Request (SAR)?
Scenario Title: What is a Subject Access Request (SAR)?
Category: Security, Verification & Data Requests
Keywords: DSAR, subject access request, SAR, data request

Answer To Give The Customer:
SARs fall under the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

A SAR can be:

Written (email, letter, webform)

Verbal (phone call, in person)

Made to any staff member — not just the Data Protection Officer

No special wording is required. If a customer says anything like “I want my data”, it may be a valid SAR.
